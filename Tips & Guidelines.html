Tips & Guidelines for Conception

Track Your Cycle

Use a calendar to track your period, fertile window, and ovulation. This helps predict your most fertile days accurately.

Maintain a Healthy BMI

A balanced weight improves hormonal balance and fertility. Consult your doctor for a healthy BMI range.

Balanced Diet

Eat a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Adequate vitamins and minerals support reproductive health.

Regular Exercise

Moderate physical activity supports fertility. Avoid excessive strenuous workouts which may disrupt your cycle.

Reduce Stress

High stress can impact ovulation. Practice relaxation techniques like meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

Monitor Hormones

Check hormone levels such as LH, FSH, estrogen, and progesterone as recommended by your doctor for optimal fertility guidance.
